Mr Chef Restrurant

Unveiled in October 2017, the restaurant is located in the epic former thermal power station of the historic town baths on Oderberger Straße. Oderberger serves fine regional cuisine with a focus on sommelier-selected German wines and specialities from across Berlin and Brandenburg.
